Cat

Cats can swim. But they don't like water.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average life span: 9.7 years
  • Favorite food: Tuna
  • Mortal enemy: Dog

The cat is one of the oldest pets of mankind. The domesticated cat belongs to the family of cats (Felidae). Cats are fast at short distances and are good climbers. In contrast to dogs they have a limited stamina.
